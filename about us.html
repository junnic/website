Our Background

Khang Shen Herbs Sdn Bhd is an advocate of natural health care products. We major in R&D in the efficacy of local traditional herbs. Our goal is to promote health benefits of Malaysian local wild ginseng and herbs. Our main focus is always on product quality starting from the sourcing of raw materials to the preparation of finished products. Each batch of finished product comes with a certified report and approval from the Ministry of Health of Malaysia. In order to meet demand, we have launched a series of local herbal products such as our Tongkat Ali series, Kacip Fatimah, Misai Kucing, Gu Shu Ling (for bone spur treatment). We will also be developing new range of products in the future. Our company’s vision is to uphold professional integrity, quality and passion. Our motto is “Customer comes first”.
